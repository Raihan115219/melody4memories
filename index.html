About Us

"Empowering Lives Through the Magic of Music"

Melody for Memories is a charity initiative where music brings hope. Through captivating concerts, our music brand raises funds to support and uplift helpless individuals in need. We believe in the transformative power of music to unite hearts, inspire change, and create lasting memories for a better tomorrow.

Teams

Our Team

  • Guitarist

    Nathan

    Nathan, born and raised in Maryland, is a passionate musician who has been playing the piano and trumpet for years. As the founder ofMelodies for Memories, Nathan believes in the transformative power of music to bring joy and connection to others. Beyond his musical talents, Nathan is an avid swimmer who finds inspiration and discipline through his time in the water. His dedication to both his craft and this project has been a driving force in its success, creating opportunities to share music and smiles with communities far and wide.

  • trumpet

    Joshua

    Joshua, a talented pianist and violist, was also born and raised in Maryland. With a love for both classical and contemporary music, Joshua has brought his creative energy and skill to Melodies for Memories, making it a vibrant and welcoming project for all involved. Outside of music, Joshua is a fencer, embracing the precision and focus of the sport, which mirrors his approach to music. He cherishes the chance to be part of a project that touches so many lives and continues to inspire others through his dedication.

  • Guitarist

    Andrew Liu

    Andrew Liu, born and raised in Maryland, is not only an exceptional tennis player but also a co-founder of Melodies for Memories. Partnering with Nathan, Andrew brought his enthusiasm and organizational skills to help build this project from the ground up. His love for teamwork—whether on the court or in the concert hall—has made him a key part of the group's success. Andrew's commitment to the project reflects his desire to spread joy through music and foster a sense of connection in the community.

  • Guitarist

    David Badmus

    David Badmus is a young sixth grader who refuses to let his age define his potential. An incredibly talented musician, David plays both the clarinet and piano, excelling in each instrument with remarkable skill and passion. In addition to his musical pursuits, David is a dedicated fencer who brings the same determination and focus to his sport as he does to music. His enthusiasm for Melodies for Memories comes from his belief that music can bridge generations and create lasting memories. David’s courage, talent, and ambition inspire everyone in the group, proving that no goal is too big when you follow your passion.

  • Guitarist

    David Gong

    David Gong is an accomplished tennis player and a valued member of Melodies for Memories. His dedication to the project stems from his love of sharing music and the connections it fosters within the community. When David isn’t on the court honing his tennis skills, he’s actively contributing to the success of the group with his energy and ideas. His collaborative spirit and enthusiasm make him an integral part of the team, as he helps bring the joy of music to new audiences and supports the project’s mission of spreading happiness through melody
